Kudos! Awards & Recognition

  • Michael Frumkin,  dean of the College of Health and Public Affairs; Nancy Ellis, director of the Center for Community Partnerships; and Rex Culp, associate dean for research and graduate studies in the College of Education, were recently recognized by Orange County Public Schools. The individuals were commended for their commitment to develop the Evans High Community School in Orlando.
  • College of Education  faculty members Vicky Zygouris-Coe, Michelle Kelley and Sherron Killingsworth Roberts have been selected to edit a prestigious national journal on literacy education. The team was handpicked by the Board of Directors of  the Association of Literacy Educators and Researchers. They will edit Literacy Research and Instruction from Winter 2012 through Winter 2015.
