2011 UCF Football Schedule Released

By UCF Athletics Feb. 10, 2011

The Knights will play six home games at Brighthouse Networks Stadium.

The 2011 UCF football schedule has been released by Conference USA, including dates for the Knights' six home games at Bright House Networks Stadium. In 2011, the UCF schedule features eight opponents who played in bowl games just last season.

You can UKNIGHT with UCF football in 2011 with a purchase or renewal of season tickets and Gold Zone parking. Season ticket information will be available Monday, Feb. 14. Tickets can be purchased and renewed online at UCFAthletics.com or by calling the UCF Athletics Ticket Office at 407-823-1000.

Sept. 3 CHARLESTON SOUTHERN (Champions Weekend)
Sept. 10 BOSTON COLLEGE (Hall of Fame Weekend)
Sept. 17 at Florida International
Sept. 23 (Fri) at BYU
Oct. 8 MARSHALL
Oct. 15 at SMU
Oct. 22 at UAB
Oct. 29 MEMPHIS (Homecoming Weekend)
Nov. 5 TULSA
Nov. 12 at Southern Miss
Nov. 19 at East Carolina
Nov. 26 UTEP (Senior Day)
Home games at Bright House Networks Stadium are capitalized.

Game dates are subject to upcoming television selections. Times of games are yet to be determined.

The reigning C-USA champions' 2011 home games at Bright House Networks Stadium include Charleston Southern (Sept. 3), Boston College (Sept. 10), Marshall (Oct. 8), Memphis (Oct. 29), Tulsa (Nov. 5) and UTEP (Nov. 26). Should UCF host the C-USA Championship Game for the fourth time in seven years, it would be played on Saturday, Dec. 3, and season ticket holders will receive the first shot at the best seats.

Highlighting the start of the 2011 home schedule are two special salutes, the first one recognizing UCF's 2005, 2007 and 2010 championship teams on Sept. 3, and the second being UCF Athletics Hall of Fame Weekend, Sept. 9 and 10. The traditional Homecoming game is tentatively scheduled for Oct. 29. The Nov. 26, game against UTEP is Senior Day. Family Weekend/College Open House will be addressed once the television schedule is announced.

In 2011, the Knights will take to the road for contests at 2010 Sun Belt champion FIU (Sept. 17), BYU (Sept. 23), C-USA West Division champion SMU (Oct. 15), UAB (Oct. 22), Southern Miss (Nov. 12) and East Carolina (Nov. 19).

For additional season ticket information or to order your tickets, log on to UCFAthletics.com or contact the UCF Athletics Ticket Office at (407) UCF-GOLD or (407) 823-1000.
