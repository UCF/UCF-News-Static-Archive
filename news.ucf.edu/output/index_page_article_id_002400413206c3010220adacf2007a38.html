Lenz, Jordan Win Employee of the Month Honors

By Mallery Laing March 15, 2005

Related Contacts and Links:

  • Print this Page

Even through the holidays, Tom Lenz and Michelle Jordan worked so hard and vigorously that they were recently named the USPS Employees of the Month for December and January.

 

Tom Lenz, Decembers Employee of the Month, is the senior purchasing agent for the purchasing department. A UCF employee since 1994, Lenz works with Physical Plant accountants to process alterations and improvements that are made all over the campus. He handles moving expenses, mail services, heavy equipment and more.

 

Previously, Lenz worked as a subcontract administrator for Hughes Aircraft. He enjoys golf and racing, and he recently went to a Mustang rally.

 

Tom is so dependable and eager to learn more of the responsibilities of the office and outside of his area, Lenzs supervisor said in nominating him. He seems to thrive on new adventures and opportunities to be more productive.

 

Lenz said its a pleasure to come to work every day because he enjoys his job and working with his colleagues.

 

Michelle Berberet-Jordan, Januarys Employee of the Month, said she loves working at the College of Engineering and Computer Science and feels very fortunate to work with such wonderful people.

 

Jordan, an information specialist, works in the Electrical and Computer Engineering and Civil and Environmental Engineering departments. She creates and writes brochures, newsletters and other promotional materials for the college. She also coordinates faculty search efforts.

 

You can always count on Michelle, her supervisor said in nominating her for the award. I consider Michelle to be a key player in the departments future success in achieving UCFs mission.

 

Before coming to UCF in 2001, Jordan wrote and edited magazines for a publishing company. She received a bachelors degree in journalism from Indiana University and is active in the alumni chapter of her sorority, Alpha Gamma Delta. She loves to cook and is active in womens organizations.
