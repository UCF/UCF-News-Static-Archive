Al Weiss Appointed to UCF Board of Trustees

By Chad Binette Feb. 28, 2005

Related Contacts and Links:

  • Tom Evelyn

    • 407-823-5988

  • UCF Board of Trustees

  • Print this Page

Al Weiss, president of the Walt Disney World Resort and chairman of the University of Central Florida Foundation Board of Directors, has been appointed to the universitys Board of Trustees.

 

Weiss, a 1976 UCF graduate, will serve a five-year term as a trustee. He was appointed last week by the Florida Board of Governors, which oversees the states 11 public universities.

 

The Board of Governors also reappointed Patrick T. Christiansen, an Orlando attorney with the Akerman, Senterfitt & Eidson firm, to a five-year term last week.

 

Weiss oversees more than 54,000 Walt Disney World Cast Members at four theme parks, two water parks, 24 resorts, a cruise line, five golf courses and other attractions. He is a UCF Distinguished Alumnus and a member of the universitys Hall of Fame.

 

A Walt Disney World Cast Member for more than 32 years, Weiss serves as chairman of the Metro Orlando Economic Development Commission and is a member of the Florida Council of 100 Board of Directors. He also serves on the SunTrust Bank Central Florida Board of Directors, the Florida Hospital Regional Council of Presidents and the Orange County Minority/Women Business Enterprise Alliance.

 

Weiss replaces Richard Lee, chief executive officer of Citizens Bank of Oviedo, on the Board of Trustees.

 

The Board of Governors announced its selections three weeks after Gov. Jeb Bush appointed Orlando attorney and UCF alumnus Hector Tico Perez to the Board of Trustees.

 

Bush also reappointed senior financial advisor Conrad Santiago to the Board of Trustees. Perezs and Santiagos appointments are subject to confirmation by the Florida Senate.

 

Perez, a partner in the Baker & Hostetler firm and a member of the UCF Foundation Board of Directors, will succeed Geraldine Ferris, a Winter Park resident and periodontist, on the board. Santiago, of Orlando, is a senior financial advisor with American Express Financial Advisors Inc.

 

The next regular meeting of the Board of Trustees is scheduled for March 31 at the UCF Student Union.
