Rosen College to Host Hospitality Career Fair

By Gert Garman Feb. 21, 2006

Related Contacts and Links:

  • Gert Garman

    • 407-903-8189

  • Rosen College of Hospitality Management

  • Print this Page

The Rosen College of Hospitality Management at the University of Central Florida will hold its Spring 2006 Career Expo from 10 a.m. to 1 p.m. Thursday, Feb. 23, in the Rosen College courtyard. More than 500 students and 60 employers are expected to attend.

Hosting one of the largest hospitality college career expos in the nation solidifies the Rosen College's place among leading hospitality institutions in terms of university/recruiter partnerships. The Rosen College campus is located in the heart of the tourist industry at 9907 Universal Blvd., Orlando.

We are fortunate to have an extremely impressive list of employers attending this event, said David Jenks, assistant director of Career Services at the Rosen College. The strong employer turnout at this expo also shows the strong interest that hospitality organizations have in recruiting our students, which is a testament to our students outstanding academic preparation and exceptional real-world experience.

Some of the employers participating in the event include Fairmont Hotels and Resorts, Hillstone Restaurant Group, Intercontinental Hotels and Resorts, The Breakers and NCL Cruiseline.

The expo is open only to Rosen College students.

Deborah Breiter, department chair for Tourism, Events, and Attractions is working with her trade show class to plan and operate the event.

The Rosen College offers programs in Lodging Management, Restaurant Management, Theme Parks and Attractions Management, Vacation Ownership Management, Finance and Accounting Management and Event Management .
