Employee of the Month Winner Oversees Interlibrary Loans

By Mallery Laing May 26, 2004

Pat Tiberii being told of her selection.

Full Photo

Related Contacts and Links:

  • Print this Page

When Pat Tiberii was called in for a meeting early this month, she had no idea it was actually a party in her honor for becoming the universitys April USPS Employee of the Month.

Tiberii has worked at UCF for more than 15 years. She began in the circulation department. For the last 10 years she has been the senior library technical assistant. She oversees UCFs lending unit, the interlibrary loan department.

On an average day in the fall, Tiberii will receive 200 to 300 loan requests. It is her job to sort and deliver photocopies and books to other schools around the world.

The Online Computer Library Center, a nonprofit, worldwide group that allows libraries to loan books from each others collections, has ranked UCF third among libraries in the state. Tiberii said she has terrific student assistants helping her, but training the students is part of her job.

Outside of work, Tiberii enjoys exercising and sometimes runs next to President Hitt at the faculty/staff gym. She likes to ride her bicycle and run marathons, and she soon will begin training for a triathlon. She also enjoys gardening and jewelry design.

Tiberii was pleased and surprised to receive the award. I was stunned. I honestly thought it was for someone else, she said.
