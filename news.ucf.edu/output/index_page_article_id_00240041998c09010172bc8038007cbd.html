UCF Shuttles to Run Later on Weeknights

By UCF Staff Jan. 27, 2005

Related Contacts and Links:

  • John Clark

    • 407-823-6687

  • UCF Shuttles

  • Print this Page

Student shuttles on all off-campus routes will run until 11 p.m. Mondays through Thursdays during February.

 

Shuttles now run until 10 or 10:30 p.m. on the off-campus routes. UCF extended the hours at the request of students whose classes end at 10:30 p.m., said John Clark, assistant director of Parking and Transportation.

 

The longer hours will start Feb. 1 and will remain in effect through at least the end of February, when UCF will determine whether there are enough riders to continue running shuttles until 11 p.m., Clark said.

 

UCF began operating student shuttles in January 2002 to reduce traffic and parking congestion on campus. Nearly 4,000 students a day are riding the shuttles during the spring semester.

 

The Central Florida Clean Air Team, a coalition of businesses and government, transportation and environmental organizations, gave UCF its 2003 Clean Air Award for reducing air pollution through the shuttle service.
