UCF To Celebrate Homecoming With Parade, Game on Saturday

By Christine Dellert Updated Nov. 5, 2008

Photo: UCF File

The Homecoming Parade begins at 10 a.m. Saturday and will travel along Gemini Boulevard.

The University of Central Florida continues its Homecoming celebrations this week, with new activities and cherished traditions for all Knights.

This year’s Homecoming Parade will wind around campus on Saturday, Nov. 8, several hours before the Knights’ 3:30 p.m. kickoff in Bright House Networks Stadium against Southern Miss.

The parade will begin at 10 a.m., starting at Central Florida Boulevard and traveling clockwise along Gemini Boulevard. Parade-goers are encouraged to arrive on campus by 9:30 a.m., and all fans need to be aware that traffic flow on campus will be heavily restricted and possibly redirected from normal flow patters from about 10 a.m. to 11:30 a.m.

To learn more about float building and see the parade route, click here.

A new Homecoming event this year, Festival of Knights, will bring together students and alumni during a four-hour tailgate Saturday, Nov. 8, on the south end of Memory Mall. The event will feature free food from Wackadoo’s and other campus restaurants, music, fortunetellers and student talent competitions.

Other events during this year’s medieval-themed Homecoming week will be a rock concert Thursday, Nov. 6, in the UCF Arena and the university’s traditional Spirit Splash on Friday, Nov. 7.

UCF kicked off the week with an effort to “go green” Nov. 1 at Bill Frederick Park in Orlando. Several hundred students took part in the clean-up efforts.

With the tagline, “It’s Our Journey,” Homecoming 2008 is dubbed “A Knight’s Quest,” with activities that will generate school spirit among new and returning Knights.

“With this theme, we’re bringing you back to a time when knights ruled, and we’ll prove that the Knights still rule today,” said Kelly Sparks, Homecoming coordinator in the Office of Student Involvement.

For more information on Homecoming events, visit http://www.ucfhc.com or the UCF Homecoming 2008 group on Facebook.

Other Homecoming events include:

-- Skit Knight begins at 7 p.m. Wednesday, Nov. 5, in the UCF Arena. For the first time, performers from SAK Comedy Lab, Orlando’s improvisation comedy group, will be hosting the event. Student organizations across campus will compete for trophies for Best Dance and Best Overall Skit.

-- The Rock Band Live tour comes to the UCF Arena Thursday, Nov. 6, for Concert Knight, with bands Panic At The Disco, Dashboard Confessional, Plain White T’s and The Cab. The show starts at 7 p.m. About 4,000 free tickets have already been handed out to students. Tickets are still available through Ticketmaster and other outlets.

-- Awarded the best university tradition in the state by Florida Leader magazine, Spirit Splash begins at noon Friday, Nov. 7, at -- and in -- the Reflecting Pond with live music from a student band and a pep rally featuring UCF’s cheerleading squad.

-- UCF alumni can still register for the annual Homecoming golf tournament at the Villas of Grand Cypress Golf Resort, starting at 7:30 a.m. Wednesday, Nov. 5. Registration is $110 for UCF Alumni Association members and $135 for non-members.

-- Celebrate the achievements of UCF alumni who have “reached for the stars,” at the annual Black & Gold Gala, which begins at 6:30 p.m. Friday, Nov. 7, in the UCF Arena. The evening includes a cocktail reception, sit-down dinner and a silent auction to benefit the UCF Alumni Trust Scholarship Fund. Cost is $60 for UCF Alumni Association members and $70 for non-members.  Corporate table sponsorships can be purchased for $750 and includes a table for 10, table sign, recognition in event program and recognition in Pegasus magazine.

Alumni are also invited to downtown Orlando next week for the Black and Gold Takeover party. The festivities begin at 9 p.m. Friday, Nov. 7, on Wall Street. Alumni Association members get in free with their membership card beginning at 10 p.m.

For more information on alumni events, visit http://ucfalumni.com.
