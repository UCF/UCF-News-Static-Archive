UCF TV Programs Now Available on Bright House On-Demand Video Service

By Chad Binette Feb. 14, 2008

Photo: Courtesy of UCF TV

"For Your Health" viewers learn about medical and health issues and receive updates on UCF's colleges of Medicine, Nursing and Health and Public Affairs.

Television programs featuring University of Central Florida faculty, students, research and cultural events now can be seen on Channel 300 of Bright House Networks’ video-on-demand service.

The new service enables Central Floridians to tune in at their convenience to UCF TV programs about a variety of topics, including seven “must-save” ecosystems in Central Florida, performances by the UCF Symphony Orchestra and how UCF laser research can enhance technology and medicine.

Up to 18 hours of programming are available on the video-on-demand channel, and shows will be changed regularly.

“By featuring our faculty and students and the activities and events on campus, we help our alumni and friends stay in touch with their hometown university,” said Richard Payne, UCF’s assistant vice president for Operations and Television Services. “This also provides a great opportunity to introduce other Central Florida residents to UCF.”

Subscribers to Bright House Networks’ digital cable service can access the on-demand programming by going to the “UCF TV” menu on Channel 300. The following programs are a sample of what viewers can find:

-- “For Your Health” viewers learn about medical and health issues and receive updates on UCF’s colleges of Medicine, Nursing and Health and Public Affairs.

-- “Zenith” highlights breakthroughs by UCF researchers in fields such as lasers, biomedical sciences and modeling and simulation.

-- “The Gallery” features the visual and performing arts through the eyes and experiences of the artists.

-- “UCF Performs” features music, theater and dance performances.

UCF TV also can be viewed online. To watch the shows, or for more information, go to www.ucftv.ucf.edu. To watch a video with highlights of UCF TV programs, click here.
