Celebrate UCF Homecoming, 40 Years

By Tom Evelyn October 20, 2003

The University of Central Florida will celebrate 40 years of academic and athletic achievements this week during KnightFest  UCF Homecoming 2003.

Check out the Homecoming Web site at http://hc.sdes.ucf.edu for the most up-to-date information on the events, including the concert, carnival, Spirit Splash and parade.

* Monday, Oct. 20 Movie Knight UCF Reflecting Pond, 8 p.m. Bring your chairs and blankets and watch "Pirates of the Caribbean, The Curse of the Black Pearl" out under the stars. FREE.

* Tuesday, Oct. 21 Black & Gold Gala Hard Rock Live, 6:30 p.m. Celebrate UCFs 40th Anniversary  From Promise to Prominence, as well as the achievements of UCF alumni who have "Reached for the Stars" at this awards banquet. The evening includes a cocktail reception (open bar) from 6:30 to 7:30 p.m. and a sit-down dinner in a delightfully elegant atmosphere. Cost is $45 for Alumni Association members and UCF faculty, staff and students and $55 for all others.

* Thursday, Oct. 23 KnightFest Golf Tournament Champions Gate, 11 a.m. registration, noon shotgun start Grab your clubs and join dozens of UCF alumni and friends at one of the largest golf tournaments in Central Florida. Cost is $75 for Alumni Association members and $95 for non-members. Price includes range balls, cart, greens fees, a box lunch during registration and an awards dinner.

* Friday, Oct. 24 Black and Gold Day Show your UCF pride by wearing black and gold to work and play. From Promise to Prominence: Celebrating 40 years UCF Library, 7:30 a.m. to 7 p.m. The UCF Library will feature UCF 40th Anniversary exhibit. Stop by the Library for a walk back in time. Spirit Splash UCF Reflecting Pond, 3 p.m. Ever want to jump in the Reflecting Pond? Nows your chance! Dont miss this wet-and-wild pep rally where everyone gets psyched up for Saturdays Homecoming game. Hullabaloo UCF Student Union, 8:30 p.m. to midnight Reunite with all your old friends and classmates at the Alumni Associations biggest event of the year. Enjoy free food and beverages, music and a great opportunity to reminisce. Havent walked around campus in awhile? The Presidents Leadership Council, starting at 7 p.m. at the Student Union, will give campus tours. Dont know what to do with the kids? Babysitters will be available on site for an additional cost per child. This event is FREE to all Alumni Association members and UCF faculty and staff. The cost is $15 for all others. The UCF Community Service Committee will also be holding a book drive at this event. Please bring books for any age group to be donated to area Community Centers. The UCF Community Service Committee will also be holding a book drive at this event. Please bring books for any age group to be donated to area Community Centers.

* Saturday, Oct. 25 UCF Downtown Open House UCF Downtown, Ying Academic Center, 36 W. Pine St., 9 to 11 a.m. Meet up with friends and university representatives to enjoy refreshments before watching the Homecoming parade. FREE.

Homecoming Parade Downtown Orlando, 11 a.m. Stake out your spot along the parade route down Orange Avenue and West Church Street. The Marching Knights will set the mood with music and the spirited parade participants will entertain everyone from kids to grandparents. FREE. KnightFest Fan Fair Tinker Field, 1 p.m. Homecoming excitement continues with food, fun and live entertainment for everyone, including the team march to the stadium. Food can be purchased from individual vendors. Homecoming Football game vs. Central Michigan Citrus Bowl, 4 p.m. Cheer on your Golden Knights as they take on Central Michigan for bragging rights in the MAC. For game tickets call the Athletic Ticket office at (407) UCF-GOLD.

For more information on Homecoming and the UCF Alumni Association, go to www.ucfalumni.com.
