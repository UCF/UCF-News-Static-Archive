Artist, PBS Show Host Donna Dewberry to Address UCF Heathrow Business Class

By Kristi Bartlett June 28, 2007

Related Contacts and Links:

  • Debbie Copertino

    • 407-531-5466

  • Donna Dewberry's Web site

  • Print this Page

Donna Dewberry, an artist known for her One Stroke painting technique and for hosting several PBS television shows, will speak at the University of Central Floridas Heathrow campus on Monday, July 2, at 1 p.m.

Dewberry will speak to Visiting Instructor Joy Colarussos Management of Organizations class about her experiences with licensing her home textile products at major retailers such as Wal-Mart, Home Depot and JC Penney and her television shows. She hosts The Donna Dewberry Show and other programs that air throughout the country on PBS affiliates.

Dewberry also will discuss how she adapts her teaching methods to different cultures, and she and the students will paint during the class.

The class will be held in the FISERV Building of the UCF Heathrow campus, 600 Colonial Center Parkway, Lake Mary. The public is invited to attend. Guests will sign in and be escorted to the classroom.

Dewberry is a self-taught artist who learned her craft through observation and practice. The author of more than 100 instructional books and videos, she created a One Stitch sewing technique and is expanding her business overseas. The enterprise involves her family; her husband serves as the marketing director, and their seven children are involved in design and management.

For more information on Donna Dewberry, visit www.onestroke.com.
