UCF Student With Passion for Physics Wins Barry M. Goldwater Scholarship

Gandhi, a Burnett Honors College student, was one of only 320 students nationwide to receive the award, which will cover up to $7,500 of the cost of his tuition, fees and books for the 2005-06 academic year.
