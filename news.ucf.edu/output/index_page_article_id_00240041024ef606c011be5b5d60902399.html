UCF College of Business Administration Co-Sponsors Entrepreneurship Fair

By UCF Staff

Related Contacts and Links:

  • Jerry Ross

    • 407-420-4848

  • Disney Entrepreneurship Center

  • Print this Page

The University of Central Florida College of Business Administration is one of the sponsors for the 3rd Annual Entrepreneurship Fair on Wednesday, Aug. 27.

The fair, hosted by the Disney Entrepreneurship Center in downtown Orlando, runs from 1 to 7 p.m. All Central Florida businesses, entrepreneurs and prospective entrepreneurs are invited to attend this free event. Other co-sponsors include the Orlando Business Journal and Florida High Tech Corridor Council.

Entrepreneurs are a vital part of the Central Florida business community, which last year was named the “best region in the country” to grow a small business by a BizJournal Newspaper survey of 55 U.S. cities. Many of those local companies are served by the 10 businesses-support organizations that are located in the Disney Entrepreneurship Center on Robinson Street.

Attendees only need to bring their business card to the fair to take advantage of three free, one-hour business seminars. The first session at 1 p.m. is entitled, “How the Best Can Get Better in Sales,” followed by “Business Insurance from A to Z” and “Web Tips, Tricks and Tactics.” Seating is on a first-come, first-served basis.

The Vendor Showcase will open at 4:30 p.m. with local vendors displaying their products and services. The event will conclude with a Networking Reception from 5:30 to 7:30 p.m.

“These events are an excellent opportunity for local businesses to discover the incredible resources that are located at the Disney Entrepreneurship Center,” said center Director Jerry Ross. “We have assembled a great group of organizations that are ready and willing to help any size business, and the Entrepreneurship Fair is a fun way to find out what is available and do a little networking, too.”

The Disney Entrepreneur Center is in its fifth year of operation and is a public-private partnership that combines 10 business support organizations into one location for Central Florida companies to access business education, one-on-one business counseling and a variety of business resources.  

Information on the event is available at http://www.disneyec.com or by phone at 407-420-4848.  The center is located in downtown Orlando, on Lake Eola, at One Landmark Center, 315 E. Robinson St., Suite 100. 

UCF Stands For Opportunity: The University of Central Florida is a metropolitan research university that ranks as the 6th largest in the nation with more than 48,000 students. UCF's first classes were offered in 1968. The university offers impressive academic and research environments that power the region's economic development. UCF's culture of opportunity is driven by our diversity, Orlando environment, history of entrepreneurship and our youth, relevance and energy. For more information visit http://news.ucf.edu.
