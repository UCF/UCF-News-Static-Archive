UCF CLOSED TODAY AS HURRICANE APPROACHES

By UCF Staff Aug. 13, 2004

Related Contacts and Links:

  • Tom Evelyn

    • 407-928-0524

  • Orange County Non-Emergency Line

    • 407-836-3111

  • Orange County Hurricane Information

  • Print this Page

In anticipation of severe weather related to Hurricane Charley, UCF offices are closed from 7 a.m. today, Aug. 13, until noon Saturday, Aug. 14.

The Rosen College of Hospitality Management, UCF Downtown and UCF South Orlando are included in the UCF Orlando closure. Regional campus offices located at community colleges are following the closing decisions of their respective community colleges.

Essential personnel may be required by their supervisors to report to work. The university opened a shelter for the families of those workers today at Sumter Hall in the Libra Community on the east side of the Orlando campus. No public shelters are open on campus at this point. For information on Orange County shelters, call 407-836-3111.

Charley, which became a hurricane Wednesday, passed the Florida Keys early today and was expected to cross near Central Florida late this evening.

Check local media outlets for updates on the storm. Weather information is also available at www.srh.noaa.gov/mlb.
