UCF Sets Another Conference USA Academic Record

By UCF Athletics April 20, 2009

Photo: Courtesy of UCF Athletics

Baseball player Chadd Hartman is one of 224 Knights on the Conference USA Commissioner's Honor Roll.

Full Photo

Related Contacts and Links:

  • Leigh Torbin, UCF Athletics

    • (407) 823-0994

  • UCF Athletics

  • Print this Page

For the third consecutive year, UCF has placed the most student-athletes of any school on the Conference USA Commissioner's Honor Roll. The 2008-09 edition released by the league office features a total of 224 Knights, eclipsing the league record of 194 that UCF established last year.

Student-athletes named to the Commissioner's Honor Roll have a cumulative grade point average of 3.0 or better. UCF's 224 outpaced SMU's 209 and East Carolina's 190 for the league high.

UCF's 224 honorees represent 60 percent of the school's total student-athletes.

Also last week, 34 UCF student-athletes received C-USA Academic Medals, symbolic of maintaining a cumulative grade point average of 3.75 or higher. That’s an increase of seven over last year’s total of 27. Overall, 10 different UCF teams can boast at least one student-athlete with at least a 3.75 cumulative GPA.

For the latest news on the Knights, log on to http://UCFAthletics.com -- the official site for UCF varsity sports. The site, which also contains ticket and Golden Knights Club donor information, is also the home of UCF's new online apparel store. Also visit http://UCFPhotos.com, the exclusive fan source for UCF action sports pictures.
