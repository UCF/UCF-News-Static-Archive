UCF Trustees, United Faculty of Florida Reach Agreement on Timing of Raises

By UCF Staff June 28, 2005

Related Contacts and Links:

  • Tom Evelyn

    • 407-823-5988

  • Print this Page

University of Central Florida faculty members and other employees who are represented by the United Faculty of Florida will receive pay increases of 3.6 percent effective Aug. 1, 2005.

 

Negotiating teams from the UCF Board of Trustees and the UFF agreed last week on the implementation of the pay increase, the amount of which was set by the Florida Legislature. Both teams wanted to ensure that all those employees represented by UFF received the raise effective Aug. 1, 2005, even if other components of salary packages remain in negotiations at that time.

 

The 3.6 percent raises will go to employees who receive ratings of satisfactory or above on their performance reviews. Employees on nine-month contracts will be eligible if they began working at UCF on or before May 7, 2005, while employees on 12-month contracts will be eligible if they started on or before June 30, 2005.

 

The UCF Board of Trustees and UFF are negotiating salary issues in the 2004-07 contract. More details about salary packages will be communicated to UCF faculty and staff as agreements are reached.

 

Other UCF employees also will receive the same raise effective Aug. 1, 2005, as outlined by the Legislature.
