Game Day Update: Spring Football Game

By Courtney Gilmartin April 10, 2008

Photo: UCF News file

The Knights will play their first spring football game at Bright House Networks Stadium at 3 p.m. Saturday, April 12.

Full Photo

Related Contacts and Links:

  • Joe Hornstein, UCF Athletics

    • 407-823-2729

  • UCF Athletics

  • Print this Page

The University of Central Florida Knights will play their inaugural spring football game at Bright House Networks Stadium on Saturday, April 12.

Gates will open at 2 p.m., and the kickoff is scheduled for 3 p.m.

All of the E lots will be open for parking, and Garage C will serve as overflow parking.  Parking for the game is free.

Lots E6, E7 and E8 will open at 8 a.m.  All other parking will open at noon. Tailgating is limited to the E lots, and it will be allowed only from noon to 6 p.m. 

To view a map of the E lot locations, go to  http://ucfathletics.cstv.com/brighthouse/goldzone-parking.html.

General admission tickets for the game are $5, and UCF students get in for free with their UCF IDs. Suites will be open as desired by suite holders, but the club level will not be open.

All eight ticket windows will be open on game day for walk-up purchases. Fans are encouraged to purchase tickets in advance in order to avoid long lines at the stadium.

Fans can purchase tickets prior to the game at www.ucfathletics.com, by calling 407-UCF-1000 and at the UCF Athletics Ticket Office at Bright House Networks Stadium. The ticket office, on the west side of the facility, is open from 9 a.m.-5 p.m., Monday through Friday.

Concessions throughout the stadium will be open, in addition to a Barnes & Noble merchandise center on the west side. Half of the stadium restrooms will be open, but the tailgate restrooms will be closed throughout the day.

Following the football game, an autograph session with the Knights football team and head coach George O’Leary will take place in the stadium’s east concourse.

At 6 p.m. UCF’s baseball team will face off against Memphis at Jay Bergman Field.  E lots by the baseball field will be open for parking. Fans with tickets to the spring football game will be admitted free to the baseball game.

For more information, go to www.ucfathletics.com.
