Nelnet Honors UCF Financial Aid Director with Full Circle Award

By UCF Staff July 15, 2005

Photo: Jerry Klein

Executive Director of Student Financial Assistance Mary McKinney

Full Photo

Related Contacts and Links:

  • Linda Gray

    • 407-823-2947

  • UCF Student Financial Assistance

  • Print this Page

A national company that provides and services student loans has honored Mary McKinney, UCFs executive director of Student Financial Assistance, with its spring Full Circle Award, a recognition of professional excellence among financial aid officers.

Nelnets Full Circle Award honors financial aid professionals who have dedicated their careers to helping students meet their educational goals through financial assistance and guidance.

Mary is a wonderful leader -- one who leads by example, and motivates others with a kindness that is exceptional, said Carmen Afghani, vice president of Nelnet Campus Solutions. She engenders loyalty from those who work for her, which in turn, produces a great team that is reliable and industrious.

McKinney has worked in financial aid for 28 years, starting as a financial aid counselor in 1977. She has led UCFs financial aid office as director since 1989 and has worked at the university for 35 years. Throughout her career in higher education, McKinney has always worked on the money side, in payroll, financing and accounting, student accounts and financial aid. 

McKinney, a first-generation college student in her family, received her bachelors and masters degrees in public administration from UCF.

I feel that all students deserve the opportunity to attend college, McKinney said. As a financial aid officer, I have an obligation to assist students in achieving that goal.

Nelnet is one of the leading education finance companies in the United States, and partners with UCF in assisting its students with loan origination and lending, and guarantee servicing.
