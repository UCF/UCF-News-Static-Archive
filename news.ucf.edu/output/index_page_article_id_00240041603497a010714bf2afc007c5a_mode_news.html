Homecoming Week Includes Dedication of Fairwinds Alumni Center

By Kristi Bartlett Oct. 28, 2005

Photo: Jacque Brund

The 23,000-square-foot Fairwinds Alumni Center houses the John and Martha Hitt Library, which will feature the written works of UCF alumni and UCF memorabilia.

The UCF Alumni Association will dedicate its new home on campus Friday, Nov. 4, during Homecoming Week festivities.

Tom Messina, associate vice president of Alumni Relations and executive director of the UCF Alumni Association, said he hopes the new building across from the UCF Arena will encourage more alumni to return to campus and strengthen their ties with the university.

The Fairwinds Alumni Center was built by alumni, for alumni, Messina said. We want to make it visible that UCF supports its alumni family. The Homecoming Parade through downtown Orlando will be at 11 a.m. Saturday.

The opening of the Alumni Center coincides with Homecoming festivities that will lead up to the Homecoming game versus Houston at 6 p.m. Saturday, Nov. 5.

The Fairwinds Alumni Center will be dedicated during a ceremony from 5:30 to 8 p.m. Nov. 4. UCF President John Hitt, Fairwinds Credit Union President Larry Tobin, UCF Alumni Association Chairman Mark Jackson and Alumni Advisory Board member Mike Manglardi will join Messina during those festivities.

Members of 4Ever Knights will give tours of the 23,000-square-foot building. All alumni and other members of the UCF community are encouraged to attend.

Fairwinds Credit Union provided the $1 million naming gift, and more than 500 alumni also donated money toward the building.

Inside the Fairwinds Alumni Center, the John and Martha Hitt Library will feature the written works of UCF alumni as well as UCF memorabilia.

In addition to being the center for all alumni events, the Fairwinds Alumni Center will be also be available for rentals. The first event held in the great hall will celebrate the 20th anniversary of an alumni couple.

For more information on the Fairwinds Alumni Center and UCF Alumni Association, go to www.ucfalumni.com . For more information on other Homecoming events, visit www.hc.sdes.ucf.edu/index.asp .
