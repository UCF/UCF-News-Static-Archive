UCF Marketing Director Accepts Job at University of Toledo

By UCF Staff Sept. 15, 2004

Related Contacts and Links:

  • Tom Evelyn

    • 407-823-5988

  • University of Toledo announcement

  • Print this Page

Jeanne Hartig, director of University Marketing and assistant vice president of University Relations at UCF, has accepted a similar position at the University of Toledo.

Hartig will begin her new job in Toledo as executive director of marketing and communications and special assistant to the president on Oct. 1. Her last day at UCF is Sept. 23.

At Toledo, Hartig will oversee marketing communications, creative services, news and information and Web strategy and will work closely with the enrollment, athletics and institutional advancement departments.

I want to thank all of my colleagues here at UCF for their support during the past five years, Hartig said. The university is now well-positioned to take strategic, integrated marketing communications to the next level.

University Marketing will continue to provide services to the UCF community under the direction of Charles Roller, associate director, and Nancy Rodriguez, assistant director.
